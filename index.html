Your dog daycare questions, answered

All about our daycare experience

Wondering how daycare works for your pup? Here’s everything you need to know to feel confident and connected while your dog enjoys their day.

What’s a day at daycare like?

From morning tail wags to afternoon naps, every day is packed with play, social time, and gentle care. Our team makes sure your dog gets exercise, rest, and plenty of love—so you can focus on your day, worry-free.

How do you ensure safety?

Your dog’s safety is our promise. We group pups by size and personality, supervise all play, and require current vaccinations. Our staff is trained to handle every situation with calm, caring expertise.

Can I visit before enrolling?

Of course! We invite you to tour our space, meet our team, and see where your dog will play. Just contact us to set up a visit—we’re excited to welcome you both.

What should my dog bring?

Bring your dog’s leash, any special food or meds, and a comfort item if you’d like. We provide cozy beds, toys, and fresh water all day long.

Care for your dog, freedom for you

Let your pup play, learn, and make new friends while you take on your day. We’ll handle the tail wags, cuddles, and care—so you can focus on what matters most.
